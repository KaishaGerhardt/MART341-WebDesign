Affordable College Student Meal!

Made by a college student for college students

As a college student, I know the plunders of finding good and affordable meals. So I found the perfect meal thats inexpensive and quick!

What you will need:

  • A Bowl
  • Tap Water
  • Ice (optional)
  • A Suffering Bank Account

How to Make:

  1. Get a bowl
  2. Fill it with tap water (tap water ranges in flavor, which will add more zest to the meal)
  3. For more flavor, add ice to the mix
  4. Sit back and enjoy!
