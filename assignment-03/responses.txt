
What is the relationship between an IP address and the DNS?

    DNS or Domain Name System associates a name with the corresponding IP Address.
    Such as giving a long-numbered IP address with the name "www.example.com".
    The computer will look up the domain name to get the associated IP address.

What is a cookie?

    Cookies are the data collected by websites to remember who you are.
    It's a number that defines who the person is logging in as or looking at the website. 

What are the three essential parts of a URL?

    The three essential parts of an URL are Protocol, Domain, and Path. 
    Protocol: the communication with a web server
    Domain: unique naming of the URL
    Path: Navigation of the directories.
